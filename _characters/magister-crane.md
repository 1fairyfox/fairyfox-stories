Magister Crane runs the Ordinal's business in Cinderwick from a quiet office above the Cage. He is
courteous to a fault, immaculately dressed, and has never in his life raised his voice — which is
precisely why people who cross him are careful never to be found. He does not think of himself as a
criminal. He thinks of himself as a *curator*: rare things, in his view, belong in careful hands,
and no hands are more careful than the Ordinal's.

Crane collects people the way he collects curios — by finding the one thing they cannot resist and
offering it, at a price they only understand later. He has had his eye on Juno Vale for years. He
does not want her caught. He wants her *useful*.

His menace is entirely in his manners: the antagonist as the smiling man who already knows how your
evening ends. He appears across the Cinderwick books and, through the Ordinal, the wider Reach.
