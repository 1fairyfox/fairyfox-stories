# Book-notes — The Cartographer of Decks (internal)

- **Author-persona:** Idris Okonkwo-Vance — cool ironist / documentarian. **Style:** plain,
  cool-literary; clean lines, emotion in the margins, strangeness landing because the reporting is
  trustworthy. Hold it: precise, understated. Sela narrates as a *report*, and keeps saying so
  ("I want to be exact about this").
- **POV:** first person past, Sela Renn, 31, third-generation cartographer; counts, measures,
  distrusts her own eyes over the record.
- **Continuity:** Ark Meridian, gen-ship, 411 years under way, 11,000 souls. The Purpose (founding
  mission, taught as gospel); founding-day; the **drift diagram** taught in schoolrooms — *Sela drew
  the current one, in 409.* Cartographers' Office; Chart Room 2 and the master chart (3 m × 5 m
  laminate). Ch.1: Deck 14-aft, frame 90→118, chart says 161 m, true 169.11 m — a wall built 8 m
  short of the hull hiding a hatch to **Deck Nought** (old observation deck; the window; the chair
  worn smooth).
- **The secret:** the ship stopped accelerating ~91 years ago and is coasting; the Office edits the
  map generation to generation as a mercy. **Ilkka Vosse**, 86, Master Cartographer, has known 41
  years and has given Sela **the key** and a month. The unlabelled folder holds **eleven** filings of
  169 m across four centuries — one of them **Sela's grandmother's**.
- **Ch.4 (new canon):** Nine-spinward, residential, resurvey. The **Tolvers**, compartment 41: Renk
  (vats, grey hands), Mira (does the corridor's accounts in her head), **Emil**, 9, who has copied
  Sela's own drift diagram by hand — *THIS IS HOW WE KNOW WE ARE GOING SOMEWHERE* — a baby not yet
  named (naming at four months), and **Bettine Tolver**, 81, dying. **The reckoning:** half the ship
  cuts a mark on the doorframe for each child on their naming-day, with the ship's cumulative
  distance (off the founding-day proclamation) cut in beside it. Bettine asks *"how far did we get,
  in me?"* Sela does the arithmetic out loud, well, without hesitating, and gives her the number —
  and cuts the mark for the baby with the boy's hand under hers. She becomes **the twelfth** not in
  Chart Room 2 but in a doorway, for free, by being kind.
- **Arc:** wants a complete correct chart; needs to decide what a true map is *for* (order vs mercy).
  Ends by drawing an honest map for one person, letting the kind story stand — as a choice.
- **Status:** ch.4 of 6 written. Ch.5 = the founders' recording; ch.6 = the chart she draws.
