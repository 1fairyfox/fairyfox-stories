# Future / Longer-term

Looser ambitions — no commitment, just direction.

- **A living world map.** As universes, characters, and cities accumulate, surface the mesh —
  a realm's cast and places, cross-book character appearances, the rare universe bridges — as
  browseable index/graph pages. The book stays the focus; this is context.
- **Cross-era encounters.** Lean into a universe spanning time periods: the seldom moment a
  character meets another from a different era of the same realm.
- **Reader polish.** Bookmarks/resume, per-book table of contents, a subtle in-book progress
  rail, all `prefers-reduced-motion`-safe.
- **Community books.** Lean into open contribution — a book submitted via PR that meets the
  blueprint + craft + integrity bar is a first-class citizen.
- **Audio / long-form exports.** Optional per-book EPUB or print-style export once a book is
  complete.
- **Deliberate larger novels** — the rare novel, done exceptionally, when a premise earns it.
